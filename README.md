# The smartest NPC AI system ever created in Garry's Mod

## Features

- **Smart cover** - Spots are scored on distance, enemy sightlines, danger zones, flank exposure and squad history. Compromised cover gets abandoned and remembered as bad.
- **Memory** - NPCs remember where they last saw you, they sweep nearby rooms (including other floors), and at some point eventually give up.
- **Squads** - They share knowledge with each other on the battlefield, they communicate through radio comms, and they have dynamic roles (Leader, Suppressor, Flanker, and Breacher) with instant leader succession.
- **Morale** - deaths, explosions and close calls shake them; pinned NPCs go to ground, broken ones run.
- **Weapon recognition** - They rush your pistol, spread out from your rocket launcher, and refuse to hold sightlines against your sniper.
- **Sound intelligence** - They can hear footsteps, doors, glass, reloads. But Suppressed weapons carry far less.
- **Formations & room clearing** - Basically ready or not kinda
- **Personalities** - every NPC rolls traits so they all fight differently.
- **Voice lines** - They actually give communications to each other.
- **Performance** - This can work with 100+ NPCs no issues

## Console

**cai_debug 1** - See every NPC state to see what they are thinking and doing.

**cai_difficulty 0.5–2** - Reaction speed and accuracy

**cai_dump** - Stats

## Settings

**If you don't want to head to console just go to options and change the settings there!**
